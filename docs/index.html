02 Four things it refuses to fake

Falsification-first, not validation-first.

The substrate is built to be attacked — by its own author first. A correct counter-example beats a clever defense. Honest downward revision of a claim is treated as a feature.

/ 01

The architectural invariant

Deterministic safety floors run before the LLM and can bypass it entirely. No LLM output overrides a floor's outcome — ever. The defense-in-depth ordering is non-negotiable.

/ 02

Append-only provenance

The Aletheia chain is Ed25519-signed and hash-linked. Truncation paths, unsigned entries, or missing prev_hash linkage fail integrity tests on contact.

/ 03

The benchmark is the legislature

VBX-ISPS-BENCH v1.1 is cryptographically committed for the v1.x major. If a candidate fails a floor, the candidate is fixed — not the benchmark.

/ 04

Honest epistemic markers

Every load-bearing claim carries Verified, Plausible, or Speculative. Markers are never stripped to make a document read more confidently.

03 The instrument · engineering blueprint

PLSS telemetry in, signed actuation out.

One pipeline. Three regimes. Every transition emits a signed chain entry, including the negative space — rejections, timeouts, and safe-passive fallbacks.

REGIME 01 · DETERMINISTIC
Hard floors
Physical-validity checks and policy floors evaluate first. The LLM does not see safety-critical inputs.
REGIME 02 · PROBABILISTIC
Nuanced band
Civium runs only when no floor fires. ACI calibrates confidence; MVCI routes by class.
REGIME 03 · DETERMINISTIC
Kernel & bus
Mercury actuates the safety-critical class with 5-cycle WCET. Privileged-write bus gates physical effect.
